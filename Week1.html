8/25/23 - On Monday we learned how to set up and access our websites. We learned some basic html codes to have a functioning website and were able to independently explore more commands to personalize our websites on Wednesday. Today, we went through a safety and engineering process slideshows which covered the importance of wearing safety equipment, how to handle supplies, and the steps in the engineering process. We also had our first challenge today. The goal of this challenge was to roll a marble from one pipe to another to a bucket. Our team won because we angled our pipes to have very slight slopes so that the marble rolled very slowly. This gave us time to pass down the pipes to the next person. We also started with our pipes pretty high up so that we had room to decline the pipe track so the marble could move forward.
