We also had our first challenge today. The goal of this challenge was to roll a marble from one pipe to another all the way to a bucket. Our team won because we angled our pipes to have very slight slopes so that the marble rolled very slowly. This gave us time to pass down the pipes to the next person. We also started with our pipes pretty high up so that we had room to decline the the pipe track so the marble could move forward.
